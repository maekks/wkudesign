THE SCHOOL

Program

The Michael Graves College is predicated on taking design and architecture to a wider public audience. It is composed of two schools, the Robert Busch School of Design and the School of Public Architecture.

The Robert Busch School of Design within the Michael Graves College at Kean University offers students programs of intense professional study created to prepare graduates with both knowledge and skills, and with the potential to achieve the highest levels of career growth. The BFA and BID are the recognized professional degrees in the studio-based programs of Advertising Design, Graphic Design, Industrial Design, and Interior Design.

The programs include extensive lecture, studio, and technology experiences, and presentations and reviews by external professional designers. In addition, students are given opportunities to pursue practical experience in the field through internships and part-time employment, to participate in events sponsored by trade organizations, and to meet design professionals during seminars and other special events, such as the world-class Thinking Creatively Conference.

Admission

Whether applying through the CommonApp or Kean University application, a portfolio of original, creative work is required for entry into the Robert Busch School of Design. Once the application is submitted, instructions for uploading the portfolio are sent through email notification.
